Spinning Out

Piece Information

  • Dimensions (in): 21 x 21
  • Materials:
    • Vinyl Record
    • CDs
    • Wood
    • Acrylic Paint
    • Paint Marker
    • Hot Glue
  • Estimated Pricing: 125$
  • Month Produced: 04/2025

Music provides Ronit Nemenman with another avenue for escapism, a way to close her eyes and let the music carry her away. Each song creates a distinct atmosphere, much like colors evoke different moods. She combined these two elements through the use of discs, each uniquely colored to match the theme of the music. The figure in the center leans back, immersed in the music, while the repeating radial symmetry draws the viewer's eye and emphasizes the sense of being carried away.
