Losing Control 2

Piece Information

  • Dimensions (in): 18 x 24
  • Materials:
    • Acrylic Paint
    • Distress Oxide
    • Ink
    • Charcoal
    • Calligraphy Pen
    • Relief Print
  • Estimated Pricing: 50$
  • Month Produced: 02/2025

Similarly to Losing Control 1, Losing Control 2 was created as a purposeful attempt to give up control. The steps in the process between the two pieces were almost identical. Looking at it, one can see how surrendering control results in a vastly different outcome.

The following includes smaller pieces that were made as experimental tests during the creation of the main work.

A testing page for the prints used to see how they would interact and how the ink would behave as a stamp.

A testing page for distress oxide was created to determine how much was too much to use, ultimately leading Ronit Nemenman to adopt a more subtle application in the final piece.
