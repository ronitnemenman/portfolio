Mark Nemenman

Piece Information

  • Dimensions (in): 24 x 18
  • Materials:
    • Canvas
    • Printed Photos
    • Acrylic Paint
    • Paint Marker
    • White Charcoal
    • Mod Podge Sealant
  • Month Produced: 02/2024

This piece was created to explore the root of Ronit Nemenman's escapism and to honor her grandfather after his passing. The background is a collage of photos spanning his entire life, over which she drew a portrait of him as she remembers him, at the age when he was her grandfather, with a slight acrylic wash to separate him from the background.
