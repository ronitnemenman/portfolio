Timed Study

Piece Information

  • Dimensions (in): 25.5 x 19.5
  • Materials:
    • Canson Paper
    • Conti Crayon
  • Estimated Pricing: 75$
  • Month Produced: 11/2024

This piece once again explores the attempt to remain anchored in the present. Ronit Nemenman created this still life in three hours, working in absolute silence. It was an exercise in removing all tools of escapism, including the planning stage she often uses, since the still life was set up by someone else. The result is a work of art minimally warped by her perception, yet still rendered in the black-and-white style she typically uses to depict reality.
