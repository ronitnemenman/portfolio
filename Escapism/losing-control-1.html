Stained Plastic (Losing Control 1)

Piece Information

  • Dimensions (in): 36 x 24
  • Materials:
    • Acrylic Paint
    • Black ink brush pen
    • White Paint Marker
    • Vellum
  • Estimated Pricing: 50$
  • Month Produced: 02/2025

In an attempt to deliberately relinquish the control and escape she usually finds in planning, Ronit Nemenman gave up control. She first used paint scraping to create a completely unplanned background. Then, she used a brush pen to find designs within the patterns and traced them into shapes. Next, she took advantage of the transparent substrate to color the backs of certain sections so that, when hung in the light, they shine like stained glass. Finally, she added opaque sections in white and small black lines to center the piece.
