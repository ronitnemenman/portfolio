Echoing Thoughts

Piece Information

  • Dimensions (in): 18 x 23.5
  • Materials:
    • Construction Paper
    • Milky Gel Pen
    • Distress Oxide
    • Paint Marker
    • Charcoal
  • Estimated Pricing: 100$
  • Month Produced: 02/2025

This piece depicts a reason why Ronit Nemenman escapes her own insecurities. When people talk about her and make comments, their words repeat over and over, echoing and becoming louder, represented by the white echoes. Eventually, their words become her own smaller thoughts as the seeds are planted in her mind, shown by the much smaller echoes originating from inside the figure.
