The Carnival

Piece Information

  • Dimensions (in): 20 x 18
  • Materials:
    • Water Color Paper
    • Water Color
    • Pelican Opaque Water Color
    • Charcoal
    • Prisma Color Pencil
  • Estimated Pricing: 100$
  • Month Produced:12/2024

The literal escaping that comes with fiction, especially books. The scene depicted is one of a carnival from Brandon Sanderson's Yumi and the Nightmare Painter. It depicted as colorful and joyful a splitting contrasts to the readers black and and white dreary reality, but not everything is so pretty as a monster known as a Nightmare lurks in the shadows ready to strike, signifying that escaping isn't the beautiful haven it is always depicted as.
