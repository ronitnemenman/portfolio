Sweetness of Prayer

Piece Information

  • Dimensions (in): 9 x 12
  • Materials:
    • Water Color Paper
    • Water Color
    • Charcoal
    • Milky Gel Pen
    • Pencil
    • Drawing Gum
  • Estimated Pricing: 40$
  • Month Produced: 10/2024

This piece was made initially to for a contest themed "The Taste of an Apple". As an Ashkenazi Jew, Ronit's first association of an apple is the sweet taste of a new year at Rosh Hoshanah and the prayer that she must say each year in both Hebrew and English.
