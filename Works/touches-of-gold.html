Touches of Gold

Piece Information

  • Dimensions (in): 4.5 x 4.5 x 4
  • Materials:
    • Ceramic Clay
    • Acrylic Paint
  • Month Produced: 05/2025

This piece is exploration of the human body stretched slighty past its proportions. While the figure looks realistic at first glance one you look again it seems contortonist and the gold highlights every strange angle.
