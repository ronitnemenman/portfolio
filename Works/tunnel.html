The Tunnel

Piece Information

  • Dimensions (in): 12 x 15
  • Materials:
    • Drawing Paper
    • Prisma Color Pencil
  • Estimated Pricing:50$
  • Month Produced:05/2023

This piece moves the viewer through the seasons as they move across the tracks, representing she shift of time but also the affect of humans on nature. Once the end of the track is reached there are no trees left to kill on stone mountains.
