Daffodil Eggstravoganza"

Piece Information

  • Materials:
    • Vinyl Record
    • Acrylic Paint
    • Hot Glue
    • Acrylic Medium
    • Foam Air Dry Clay
    • Wood
  • Month Produced: 03/2024

This surrealist piece pictures a civilization built on a daffodil, with an eggo yolk at its center. Each petal features a different way the people interact with the flower including: ice climbing, tubing, swimming on the beach, dying in a tsunami, and harvesting the yolk for food.

The following is a collection of photos of the sculpture taken from more angles

The following is a collection of process and progress photos taken during the creation of this piece along with explanations

First the vinyl record was cut into the shape of a flower using a laser cutter.

Using a small glass bowl and a heat gun the vinyl was then warped into the three d shap of a flower

Using a piping bag and washes for shading, Ronit was able to use acrylic medium to make three dimensional waves and icicles

Hot glue was then added to give the yolk mits shiny effect along with the tiny residents of the city.
