Concentric Flood

Piece Information

  • Dimensions (in): 9 x 12
  • Materials:
    • Drawing Paper
    • Relief Print
  • Estimated Pricing: 30$
  • Month Produced: 02/2024

This piece looks into the turmoils and beauty created by nature. While the trees die from the rains, the fish gain a new home to live in and thrive. The two live in opposite worlds that only thrive with the other's death.
