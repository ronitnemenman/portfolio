Ronit Nemenman is a high school senior and a mostly two dimensional artist based in Atlanta, Georgia. Her works vary in materials, but she works largely in charcoal and water soluble media, to convey to others how she sees the world around her from both an emotional standpoint, and one of growth as she expands her view with experience.
